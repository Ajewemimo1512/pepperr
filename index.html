Harvest Mechanics

How to Claim PEPPER Community Token

1.

Connect your Wallet:

You'll need to connect your wallet in order to claim PEPPER

2.

Wait for the harvest season

During pre-harvest, PEPPER will grow based on the amount of CHZ you have staked on the Chiliz Chain via the Governance. Once the harvest season begins you will be able to farm your PEPPER.

3.

Time to Harvest!

Make sure to harvest the PEPPER that grew from the pre-harvest period before the deadline and prepare for the next harvest season! There will be multiple harvest seasons, as long as the PEPPER supply lasts! Don’t be a lazy farmer and come back every day!
